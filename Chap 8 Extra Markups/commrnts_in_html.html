PIAIC offers the following cources

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Blockchain Computing
  • Internet of Things

Artificial Intelligence

The artificial Intelligence program of the PIAIC takes the student through the foundation of AI technology via teaching programing in a open source languages like Python, Numpy, Pandas, Tensorflow 2.0 and Flask, and taeching deploying practical AI solutions and use cases, and ending with real-life examples of medern day AI in practice.








Cloud Native Computing

The Cloud Native Computingprogram prepares the student to program in open source technologies like LInux, Doker, Kubernates, JavaScipt, Node.JS, and React Native while introducing student to development of Microservices and softwere development techniques on cloud platforms like AWS.








Blockchain Computing

The main emphasis of blockchain program is to understand blockchain like Ethereum and Hyperledger (Fabric) and learn to prograam in languages such as Solidity, JavaScript on frame work like Node.JS. The program also introduces them to development of Distributrd Apps (DApps), frameworks like Web3 and Truffle and in-depth understanding of (BaaS) and how to develop and offer an Initial Coin Offering (ICF) of a crypto-token.








Internet of Thing

The internet of thing introduces the student to open source development technologies like Rust, Embaded, Rust, WebAssembly, Hyper, Diesel, Google Assistance, and Kubernates while making them efficient in developing Microservices for IoT Edge, IoT Cloud, and voice computing for device computing.
